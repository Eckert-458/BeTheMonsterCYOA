A monster's strength is determined by POWER, MAGIC, and SPEED. Theses stats go from 1 to 11.

POWER: Physical Strength, resilience, and toughness
MAGIC: Magical prowess, and resistance to magical effects
SPEED: Cunning, reflexes and quickness of thought

You begin with 1 in each stat, and must end with a value between 1 and 11 (inclusive) in each stat.

Power:
1 - Almost the complete lack of the stat
3 - An average human
5 - A master in the stat
7 - The realm of heros
9 - The realm demi-gods and gods
11 - Surpassing the gods themselves

Body Parts:

Based on the body type you select, you will gain a number of horn, mien, maw, arm, wing, leg, and tail slots. These slots may be used to gain increases to ability score or useful abilities. Slots that remain empty will either not offer extra functionality or not manifest on your final physical form at all. Multiple slots of the same type may manifest as either additional body parts or as additional abilities for existing body parts. If you want additional slots or if your chosen form doesn’t normally include a particular body part, you may purchase an additional slot.

Note: Brood Monsters and Demi-Monsters can exchange their forms maw slots for arm slots
